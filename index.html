MassMesh is a community owned and operated mesh network consisting of many nodes spread throughout the city.

Learn More What is a mesh?

Our Mission Is Simple

We aim to create a free, resilient, stand-alone communication system that serves both for daily use and also for emergencies—be it power outages or internet disruption—running software that helps our community with hyperlocal maps and events.

An Open, Neutral Community

Unlike traditional internet infrastructure, all participants are encouraged to extend the network to reach a wider audience. There are no gatekeepers to get permission from as the network belongs to everybody.

Join Our Growing Network

Whether you are a building owner, someone with rooftop access, or just live near other people, we need your help! Join our mailing list, chatroom, or attend a meeting to learn how you can participate.
