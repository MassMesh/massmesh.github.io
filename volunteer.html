Distributed Internet by the People

Mass Mesh is a high-tech social club building community-owned mesh networks throughout Boston.*

* Not living in Massachusetts? Find your nearest community-owned network on Join The Mesh (dot) Net.

Access Point Operators Wanted!

We're looking for volunteers willing to share their Internet connection with their neighbors wirelessly and let us conduct semi-regular testing at their home. Our technology is brand new, and in desperate need of real-world testing. Any extra monthly Internet costs on your end will be covered by us, but interested volunteers should be ready to spend about $150 on hardware.
Please fill out this form if you're interested in volunteering as an access point host.
